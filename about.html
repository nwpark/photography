About

Born in Bath City, Somerset, 1993. I was given my first camera at the age of 9. I decided to pursue Photography after studying fine art, photography and theatre studies at Alevel I continued onto University and studied Fashion Photography at Southampton Solent University which I will graduated from towards the end of 2015.

Much of the work that is featured on my website is work I did for my university projects and work in my own time. I like to make each shoot I do very different which is why I have experimented with incorporating art into my work as well as fashion and landscape. I like to make sure I have an equal amount of studio shots aswell as on location to show off my capability to achieve both with great pictures.

I have been very fortunate enough to work for Emilio De La Morena who designs high fashion Cocktail dresses; I had the opportunity to photograph all his collections from 2013-2014 and Photograph backstage at his show for London Fashion week as well as the Catwalk.

I am currently about to finish my Fashion Photography Degree and hope to pursue in the industry, it would be great to work for a big designer again and work my way up in the industry, I am currently living in Southampton.
